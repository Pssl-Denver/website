Parent Survivors of Suicide Loss

Free support group in Denver for parents who have lost a child to suicide

We are a peer led support group open to any parent who has lost a child to suicide. We meet every third Tuesday of the month from 7-9pm at Bethany Lutheran Church, 4500 E. Hamden Ave Cherry Hills, CO 80113 located near I-25 and Hamden Ave.

We are currently meeting on zoom due to covid-19. Please contact:
